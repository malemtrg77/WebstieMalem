About Me

Throughout my time in the MBKM (Merdeka Belajar Kampus Merdeka) program, I was privileged to be part of a transformative journey in education. This program allowed me to step beyond the confines of traditional learning and immerse myself in the world of teaching, mentorship, and community engagement. Working alongside passionate teachers, supportive peers, and enthusiastic students taught me lessons that extend far beyond textbooks

The MBKM experience was more than just a teaching assignment; it was a journey of self-discovery, growth, and meaningful connections. I carry forward these memories and lessons with immense gratitude, as they have shaped my perspective and purpose in life. These moments, captured in photos and friendships, serve as a constant reminder of the importance of dedication, resilience, and the joy of giving back to the community
